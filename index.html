WORK

Stylist Best Beauty - A CMS driven digital platform to show off all of the products featured in Stylist Magazines 2016 Best Beauty Awards.

National Burger Day - Reaching over 1500 active users at its peak, this website needed to handle high traffic on the day. As well as letting users get tickets to the big event.

Runaway Helpline - Built for the charity Missing People, this site needed to give young people advice on what they could do if they are considering running away from home or have already ran away.

NCS Ingeus - Built to help young people learn new skills, meet new people and find new opportunities in their local community.

Diabetes UK - A native iOS app that toured the UK in a bus to help people determine whether they were at risk of Type 2 diabetes. If they were, it AirPrinted a PDF of their results to take to their doctor.

One Petition App - A native iOS app that let One members collect signatures from the public. The main challenge was that it needed to work offline, and then let One workers sync up the data when they had WiFi.

OTHER TECH BITS

Facebook Instant Articles / Apple News - Both of these platforms were released during my time at Shortlist Media. As a publisher they wanted to be at the forefront of digital publishing and it was my job to integrate them. These efforts were successfull as 2 weeks after launching Facebook IA we saw a 120% growth in online traffic.

Prototyping - Part of my job as Creative Technologist at Shortlist was experimenting with new technologies and ideas. These included prototyping a potential React Native app for a live event. Also a project where we created a React site that generated articles from the top trending subreddits of the day.

Open Source - Part of my role as a codebar organiser is maintaining the repos. Everything we have built is Open Source, from the planner to the tutorials. We encourage students to contribute and to help with this we run "24 Pull Requests" (see below) every December. I have also created tutorials and guides to aid new developers, such as several Intro to Python tutorials and an everyday git command guide.
